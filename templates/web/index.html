About Brothers Group

Brothers group is a 700 Cr + Business conglomerate spread across south India and Middle east. The group is into Cement – Distributor & C&F, Steel Dealership, Transportation & Logistics, Petroleum & Gas, Dealerships of the major brands of Vehicle Tyres, Shopping Malls, E- Commerce business.

Though the world has changed drastically in the last 40 years since the groups inception, the tradition and the values are followed till date and Brothers group has grown from a small town shop to build an empire in north Kerala. The group owes its ever–growing popularity to its never changing motto ‘Driven by its entrepreneurial Spirit, Brothers group will diversify into high growth sectors while gaining leadership in the industries it is already a part of, in an ethical and socially responsible way.
